What we do

You got idea for an app and discuss it with us.
Our process starts from there

1.

Research on your idea, finding out if it is unique or existing and finally completing our basic market research on it.

2.

Now we move to ideation phase followed by Ul UX designs of your app in Figma.

3.

If your idea is funded (self-funded or Investor) then we move to the development phase in subsequent stable versions ready for launch.

4.

Incase of not being funded, we help you with Proof of Concept which is a design prototype so that you can show it to potential investors to raise investments.

5.

After app launch, we manage your app as a tech partner. And we keep a close look into your existing or emerging competitors to stay ahead of them.

6.

Once your app sustains business operations, we build a dedicated tech and marketing team to scale with new features and innovations.
